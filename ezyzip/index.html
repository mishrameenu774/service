Our Services

The services section of an E-Cell (Entrepreneurship Cell) society website should highlight the key offerings that support budding entrepreneurs, startups, and students interested in entrepreneurship.

Personalised Mentorship

Offer one-on-one or group mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs, alumni, or industry experts. Showcase profiles of mentors and their areas of expertise.

Workshops & Seminars

Regular workshops on business fundamentals (e.g., fundraising, marketing, scaling).Invite industry leaders and entrepreneurs for webinars on specialized topics.

Startup Funding

Connect startups with VCs, angel investors, or crowdfunding platforms. Share info on pitching sessions and business plan competitions for funding.

Incubation Support

Provide information on incubation services such as workspace, internet, and access to other resources. Partner with startup incubators to provide members a space to develop their ideas.

Networking Opportunities

Host networking events like mixers, meetups, or demo days for members to connect with investors, mentors, and peers. Partner with entrepreneurial groups and local businesses to expand the network.

Competitions and Challenges

Host business idea or startup pitch competitions, hackathons, or case study challenges.Showcase past competition winners and upcoming events.
